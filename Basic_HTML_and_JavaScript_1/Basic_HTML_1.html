Tokidoki, which translates to “sometimes” in Japanese, is an internationally
recognized and iconic lifestyle brand based on the vision of Italian artist Simone
Legno and his partners, serial entrepreneurs Pooneh Mohajer and Ivan Arnold.
Since debuting in 2005, tokidoki has amassed a cult-like following for its
larger-than-life characters and emerged as a sought- after global lifestyle brand.
