The Twilight Saga

Yet another vampire story targeting the younger enthusiasts, the Twilight Saga enthralled adults as well, all over the world. Twilight starts with Bella, a senior in high school, who almost immediately falls in love with Edward, a vampire attempting to blend in with the rest of the high school kids at a small town high school in Forks, WA.
